UV-C Configurations

VX-One S comes in different configuration options. The models range from 110W to 880W UV-C output. Higher the output, longer the radiation range and shorther the disinfection cycle. All models are upgradable by purchasing additional lamps and wings.

UV-C Effectiveness

The VX-One S UV-C radiation levels where measured by The Institute of Occupational Safety in Slovenia, Ljubljana. The measurement reports are available here.

Included with the unit are certified UVC dosimeters that changes color to visibly demonstrate three energy levels of UV-C exposure (254 nm) at 25, 50 and 100 mJ/cm2. The dosimeters can be placed on the objects to verify the radiation amount. A study has shown that SARS-CoV-2, the virus that causes COVID19, can be inactivated by UV-C at a dose of 22 mJ/cm2. Reaching a dose of 50 and 100 mJ/cm2. More information on UVC dosimeters.

Certifications

The VX-One S is certified for use in the hospitality sector and critical government institutions. It's been tested by the independent Slovenian Institute of Quality and Metrology for the following standards: EN 303 446-1 (55015), EN 60598-1:2015+A1, EN 60598-2-:2018, and EN 62471:2008.
